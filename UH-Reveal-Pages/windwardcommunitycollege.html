E KOMO MAI! WELCOME TO WCC

Welcome to our campus in Kāneʻohe, located right at the foot of the stunning Koʻolau mountains! WCC is a two-year college focused on providing strong transfer paths to UH Mānoa and unique career-focused programs like Veterinary Technology, the Marine Option Program, and Palikū Theatre.

Our beautiful, small campus offers an intimate and focused learning environment perfect for hands-on experience, whether you're working with animals, studying the Kāneʻohe Bay marine life, or preparing for the stage. Get ready to explore the windward side's unique opportunities!

-From the Windward CC Admissions Team
