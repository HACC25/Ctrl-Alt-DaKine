ALOHA PŪ! WELCOME TO UH HILO

I'm excited to guide you through life here at the University of Hawaiʻi at Hilo! Located in the vibrant, rainy side of Hawaiʻi Island, UH Hilo offers a unique environment where the ocean, the rainforest, and the volcano meet. We are a small, tight-knit campus known globally for our research in **Marine Science**, **Astronomy**, and the **Daniel K. Inouye College of Pharmacy (DKICP)**.

From our state-of-the-art Science & Technology building to the tranquil grounds of the main campus, you'll be learning in a living laboratory. Dive in and explore the difference of a four-year education in the heart of the Pacific.

-From the UH Hilo Office of Admissions
