E KIPA MAI! WELCOME TO KAUA'I CC

I'm thrilled that you're exploring what life is like here at Kauaʻi Community College! Located in beautiful Līhu‘e, Kaua‘i, KCC has been the foundation for success on the Garden Isle since 1965. We're known for our strong Hospitality and Sustainable Technology programs and our seamless transfer paths to the UH four-year campuses.

From the welcoming atmosphere of the Campus Center to our specialized labs for Culinary Arts and Tropical Agriculture, you'll join a diverse community dedicated to learning and innovation. To give you a better feel for our home, here are a few snapshots of campus life.
