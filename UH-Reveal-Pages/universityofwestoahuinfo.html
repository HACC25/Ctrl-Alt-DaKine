E LALAU PŪ! WELCOME TO UH WEST OʻAHU

Welcome to our campus in Kapolei, the heart of Oʻahu's Second City! UH West Oʻahu (UHWO) offers a unique four-year educational experience focused on professional career paths, community engagement, and distance learning flexibility. We are committed to high-demand fields like Cybersecurity, Creative Media, and Sustainable Community Food Systems.

Our modern, state-of-the-art facilities, like the Creative Media Facility and the Noʻeau Center, provide an intimate learning environment on the beautiful West Side of the island. Get ready to forge your path in a truly distinct academic community!

-From the UH West Oʻahu Admissions Team
