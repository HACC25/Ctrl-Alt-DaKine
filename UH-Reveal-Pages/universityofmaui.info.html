E KOMO MAI! WELCOME TO UH MAUI COLLEGE

Welcome to the Valley Isle! Located in Kahului, UH Maui College is known for its practical, hands-on training that fuels Maui’s workforce. We specialize in **Culinary Arts**, **Hospitality**, **Sustainable Agriculture**, and vital trades like **Automotive and Electronics**.

Your learning experience here is closely connected to the community, offering real-world application, whether you are preparing a feast in the Pāʻina Building's kitchens or learning cloud computing in the Kaʻaʻike facility. Get ready for a day that combines strong academics with the vibrant life of Maui.

-From the UH Maui College Office of Admissions
