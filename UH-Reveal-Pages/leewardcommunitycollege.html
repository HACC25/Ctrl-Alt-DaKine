A Personal Welcome to Mānoa

I'm thrilled that you're exploring what life is like here at the University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa. We aren't just a university; we're an ohana(family) and a world-class research institution set in the lush Mānoa Valley. Founded in 1907, our campus offers a unique blend of rigorous academics and unparalleled natural beauty.

From the historic trees of McCarthy Mall to our state-of-the-art labs, you'll be joining a diverse community of scholars, leaders, and innovators. To give you a better feel for our home, here are a few snapshots of campus life.
