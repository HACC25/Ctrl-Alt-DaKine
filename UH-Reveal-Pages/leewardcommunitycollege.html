E KIPA MAI! WELCOME TO LEEWARD

I'm thrilled that you're exploring what life is like here at Leeward Community College! Located in beautiful Pearl City, LCC has been the foundation for success on the West side of O‘ahu since 1968. We're known for our strong Career & Technical Education programs and our seamless transfer paths to the UH four-year campuses.

From the welcoming atmosphere of the Campus Center to our specialized labs for Automotive and Culinary Arts, you'll join a diverse community dedicated to learning and innovation. To give you a better feel for our home, here are a few snapshots of campus life.
